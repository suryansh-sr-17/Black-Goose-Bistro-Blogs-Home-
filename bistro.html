Our Exquisite Recipies :

1. Tapende (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties. My father recommends :

"Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature just before you serve it. In the cold shivering winters,serving it warm is the best choice."

The Ingredients are :
  • 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers
Instructions :
  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend unitl as smooth as possible.
  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times untl they are incorporated, but still retain some texture according to your liking.
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional).
